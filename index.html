About me

As an AI/ML Engineer, I design and build end-to-end machine learning systems that translate research into real-world impact. I specialize in multimodal and generative AI, combining deep learning, model optimization, and scalable engineering to create solutions that are efficient, reproducible, and production-ready.

My experience includes developing full ML pipelines — from data preprocessing and feature engineering to model training, evaluation, and deployment — with a strong focus on reliability, performance, and maintainability. I have worked with modern ML stacks including PyTorch, Hugging Face, computer vision frameworks, and containerized deployments.

My work spans computer vision, large language models, and domain-focused AI systems such as healthcare and life sciences. I place strong emphasis on measurable outcomes, reproducibility, and system efficiency, and I enjoy collaborating across research and engineering teams to deliver intelligent systems that scale in production.
